With access only to step(), the transition dynamics(T) and reward function(R) are hidden. 
I observed that Value Iteration and Policy Iteration do not work directly anymore, 
because both methods require the full MDP model.

In my implementation, Value Iteration uses env.transition_matrix and env.get_reward_per_action(), 
and Policy Iteration also needs transition probabilities and rewards for its Bellman updates. 
The step() function only returns sampled transitions and rewards, but not the full model.

Therefore, both methods cannot be applied directly in this setting. 
They would only work if the dynamics and rewards were first estimated from sampled data.
